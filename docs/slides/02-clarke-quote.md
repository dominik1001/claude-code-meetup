# Slide 02 — The Clarke Quote

## Position in Arc
The opening frame. Establishes the metaphor for the entire talk.

## Visual
- Background: near black `#080808`
- Large dimmed gold quotation mark top-left (decorative, 140pt)
- Candle flame illustration — bottom center (same as slide 1, continuity)

## Text

**Quote:** *Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic.*
**Attribution:** — Arthur C. Clarke, 1973

## Spoken Words
Read the quote slowly. Pause after it lands.
Then move to the next slide without commentary — let the flip do the work.

## Timing
~30 seconds

## Design Notes
- Quote in Georgia italic, 34pt, cream
- Attribution in Calibri, 16pt, gold
- The large decorative quote mark in gold-dim creates depth without competing
- Candle connects visually back to slide 1 — audience won't consciously notice yet

## Why This Slide Exists
It gives the audience a shared reference point before you flip it.
Everyone knows Clarke. Using it signals you're going somewhere serious — not just a product demo.
